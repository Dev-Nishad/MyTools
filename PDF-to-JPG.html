Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF to JPG converter free?

Yes, our converter is 100% free with no hidden costs. You can convert unlimited PDFs without any restrictions.

Are my PDFs uploaded to a server?

No! All processing happens locally in your browser using PDF.js. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

What's the maximum PDF size I can convert?

There's no strict limit, but very large PDFs (100+ pages) may take longer. For best performance, we recommend PDFs under 50MB.

Can I convert only specific pages?

Yes! After uploading, you'll see previews of all pages. Click to select/deselect individual pages before converting.

What image quality should I use?

For web use, 75-85% offers a good balance of quality and file size. For printing, use 90-100% with higher DPI settings.

Can I get PNG instead of JPG?

Yes! Set the background color to "Transparent (PNG)" to output PNG files instead of JPG.
