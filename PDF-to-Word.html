About this PDF to Word tool

This online PDF to Word tool is designed as a front‑end‑only, privacy‑friendly converter demo. It reads your PDF in the browser and generates a DOCX file that summarizes the uploaded document.

  • Upload any PDF file from your device.
  • We detect page count and basic file information in‑browser.
  • Select an optimization level to control DOCX size.
  • Download a Word document that reflects your chosen options.

For a production‑ready PDF→Word conversion (with full text & formatting), you would typically integrate a backend service or third‑party API. This page focuses on UI, SEO, AdSense placement, and safe client‑side processing.
