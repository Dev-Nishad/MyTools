Why Use Our Online Image Compressor?

Website speed is a critical factor for SEO rankings. Large image files slow down your page load time, increasing bounce rates and hurting your user experience. Our online image optimization tool allows you to compress JPG, PNG, and WEBP files right in your browser.

Key Features

  • Secure & Private: All compression happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. No images are uploaded to a server.
  • Lossy & Lossless Compression: Adjust the quality slider to find the perfect balance between file size and visual quality.
  • 100% Free: No hidden costs, watermarks, or sign-up requirements.

How to Optimize Images for SEO

Search engines like Google prioritize fast-loading websites. By reducing your image file sizes by up to 80%, you significantly improve your Core Web Vitals score. Simply upload your image, select the quality level, and download the optimized version instantly.
